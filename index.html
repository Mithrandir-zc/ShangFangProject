WELCOME to Mountain ShangFang

Located in the southwestern part of Beijing, Shangfangshan National Forest Park is a stunning natural reserve renowned for its picturesque landscapes and cultural heritage. Covering an area of over 7,000 hectares, the park is a haven for nature enthusiasts and history buffs alike.

The park is famous for its unique karst landforms, including numerous caves, cliffs, and peaks, which offer breathtaking views and exciting exploration opportunities. Among these natural wonders, Shifo Cave and Baigu Cave stand out as must-visit attractions due to their impressive stalactites and stalagmites formations.
